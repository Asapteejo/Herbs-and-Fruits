Growing notes

Short, useful guidance for customers who want plants to thrive.

This section works better as compact, readable advice instead of novelty alerts. The tone is still approachable, but the presentation is more polished and easier to trust.

Reading guide

Think of these as quick field notes.

Each post keeps to a narrow topic: what the plant likes, what people usually get wrong, and one practical tip worth applying immediately.

March 15, 2025

Getting rosemary established in strong sun

Rosemary performs best when it is not treated like a thirsty leafy vegetable. Good drainage, enough airflow, and restraint with watering matter more than constant attention.

Quick tip

Use a light soil mix and let the top layer dry before watering again.

March 10, 2025

Pomegranate care for growers who want fruit, not just foliage

Pomegranate prefers full light, moderate watering, and patience. It rewards consistency more than overfeeding or frequent repositioning.

Quick tip

Keep the root zone from staying soggy for long periods, especially in containers.

March 5, 2025

Mint grows fast, but that does not mean it should take over everything

Mint is productive and forgiving, but it spreads aggressively. Treat it as a plant that benefits from boundaries and regular trimming.

Quick tip

Grow mint in a separate container if you want to avoid it crowding slower herbs.
